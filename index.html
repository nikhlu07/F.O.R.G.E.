F.O.R.G.E. operates on three interconnected systems, each engineered to eliminate loopholes and enforce transparency from start to finish.

Fund Allocation Matrix

  • Smart Contracts: Self-executing funds that eliminate middlemen.
  • Geofenced Funds: Locked to specific projects, not private jets.
  • Milestone-Based Release: Payments triggered by verified progress.
  • Auto-Clawback: Instantly recovers funds if fraud is detected.

Neural Transparency Grid

  • Public Ledger: Every transaction is broadcast and immutable.
  • Zero-Knowledge Audits: Full compliance without revealing sensitive data.
  • Citizen Verification: Community nodes ensure decentralized oversight.
  • AI Fraud Detection: Proactively identifies and flags corruption patterns.

Reality Anchoring System

  • Oracle Integration: Connects smart contracts to real-world data.
  • DePIN Verification: Proves physical infrastructure actually exists.
  • Biometric Access: Ensures only authorized individuals can access funds.
  • Supply Chain Tracking: End-to-end documentation from source to delivery.
